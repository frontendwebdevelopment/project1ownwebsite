10 Most Popular Places Around Bengaluru

Welcome to Bangalore the IT capital of India or how we fondly call it 'Namma Bengaluru' ! A variety of places in and around Bangalore, provides its first time visitors with such an exhilarating impression that everybody falls for its charm. A beautiful blend of delightful sights and appealing destinations this stunning abode of dreamers has much more to offer when it comes to weekend getaway around Bangalore.

Rejuvenate yourself along with family and friends by exploring the diverse activities and mesmerizing scenic locations the city proffers. Treat yourself to fun and informational food and wine tours. Enjoy the thrill of trekking at Savandurga, Kaballadurga, Kunti Betta and many more places. Cycle in the Ghats of Karnataka, experience the freedom from pollution and traffic cycling from Bidadi to Magadi and several other routes.

Mesmerize yourself at the Hogenakkal and Shivanasamudra waterfalls. Spend luxurious day outs with loved ones at some of the best resorts in and around Bangalore. Go go-karting, Micro light flying and Quad biking to satisfy the daredevil within. Cherish innumerable romantic moments with your partner dining at the rooftop, star gazing and take pleasure in several other activities.
1. Antaragange Caves Night Trek

Antaragange is situated at a distance of 70 km from Bangalore. Antaragange is an excellent example of natures creativity. Strewn with rocks and boulders, Antaragange has some excellent caves that have formed from volcanic rocks.

So if you are fond of cave exploration and trekking, Antaragange is the perfect place for a weekend getaway. With a fairly easy terrain for trekking and cave exploration, Antaragange offers an enjoyable weekend in the lap of nature.

2. Kunti Betta Trekking

Situated around 130km from the heart of the city, Kunti Betta is also one amongst the popular places around Bangalore and a hub for the adventure seekers; especially for the trekkers. Though the vicinity boasts of its unique beauty and appeal, its allurement gets double-fold during the nights.

It is during this period, trekkers from different places throngs Kunti Betta and embarks on a night trek expedition to the peak and satiates their carvings for fun and adventure.

3. Bheemeshwari Forest Camp

Want to spend some time in a calm and serene environment? Escape into the woods! The Bheemeshwari forest camp offers great scenic beauty and tranquility. If you are a nature lover, you could indulge in nature walks and spend some peaceful time exploring and sightseeing in the forest.

If you are an adventure enthusiast, you can go for trekking or participate in adventure activities. Thrillophilia can arrange for stay in comfortable jungle lodges if you intend to spend a day or two in the peaceful atmosphere at Bheemeshwari.

4. Wildlife Safari Bannerghatta National Park

Feel like escaping into the woods to forget all worries? Lose yourself in the beauty and serenity of the Bannerghatta National Park. Situated only 22 kms from Bangalore, the Bannerghatta National Park is a haven for nature lovers. Lush green vegetation and rich biodiversity make the national park a favourite among nature lovers.

Spread over 25,000 acres, the Bannerghatta National Park is one of the richest, natural biodiversity reserves. The national park is home to wildlife animals ranging from tigers to butterflies. An ideal location for a quick getaway from the city and relax in the tranquility of the wilderness.

5. Visit to Shivanasamudra Waterfalls

Situated on the banks of the River Cauvery, the Shivanasamudra waterfalls are formed due to splitting of the river around ravines and rocks of the Deccan Plateau. The river also forms the Shivanasamudra Island during its course.

The Shivanasamudra waterfalls attract tourists from all over the country and the world because of its scenic beauty. Refreshing and relaxing, the Shivanasamudra waterfalls are the perfect location for a quick weekend getaway.

6. Gokarting and Paintball

For those who like driving fast cars and racing with friends, go karting in Bangalore is the best way for you to beat the heat and engage in some exhilarating fun! Paintball is a fun, grueling and energy-driven game packed with adventure and excellent team building adventure for you and your friends! So grab your buddies and head for unlimited fun!

7. Camping in Coorg

Snuggled deep in the pockets of Western Ghats, Coorg, the home of the Kodavu people, will claim your imagination! Just a short drive of about 250km will bring you to this charming abode of greens and hilly escapes..

Camping in the inviting greens of Coorg is surely an experience that has to be felt to be believed, the calming cool drafts of air will fill your lungs and the morning brings with it a gorgeous sunrise amongst refreshing environs of the coffee plantations. Coorg, is a fairytale destination that comes alive right before your eyes!.

9. Spend a Cool Evening at Snow City in Bangalore

Enjoy cavorting in pure snow right in the heart of Bangalore with Snow City. Covering an extent of 12,500 square feet, the park encompasses an area of 33 feet high snow cover. Avail of this 45 minutes experience anytime between 10 am to 8 pm. You will be provided with protective gear of warm jackets, boots, gloves and caps/hoods. First, go through the entry lounge which has varying temperatures between 18° C - 24° C, and 5°C - 15° C, to acclimatise yourself with the extreme temperatures in the snow area.

After that, a whole new world awaits - frolic around in the snow, go snow rock climbing, slide on snow mounds, or participate in snow rafting. Make snow balls and take aims at your friends. With temperatures between -5° C to -15° C, up to 10 cubic feet of pure snow is created by the pumping of 5,000 litres of filtered water by snow machines.

10. Trek to Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills or Nandidurg is one of the most popular tourist places around Bangalore. Situated at a distance of 60 km from the capital city, the Nandi hills are a favourite destination among people looking for a quick getaway location to let off some steam and relax.

There are a number of tourist destinations in and around Nandi Hills. Very few know that there is a trekking trail to the Nandi hill peak. With clean, cut out steps, the climb uphill is fairly easy and takes you through a route flanked by lush green trees. The view from the slopes and the peak is breathtaking and completely refreshing.
